Drones in Agriculture

Nutrient Deficiency Detection System for Agricultural Drones

Nutrient deficiency in soil and crops is a primary factor affecting agricultural productivity. Timely detection of deficiencies like nitrogen, potassium, zinc, magnesium, and iron ensures healthier plants and higher crop yields to the farmer. We specialize in using light weight Deep Learning models on IoT edge devices deployed in agricultural drones for accurate and real time diagnosis of diverse range of crops. This will improve the soil health and enhance crop quality with a reduction in overall and costs associated with overuse of fertilizers, etc.

Crop Disease Detection System for Agricultural Spraying Drones

Crop diseases pose a significant threat to global food security, often leading to massive losses for farmers. Traditional methods of disease detection rely on manual inspections, which are time-consuming and prone to errors. We specialize in using light weight Deep Learning models on IoT edge devices deployed in agricultural drones for accurate and real time diagnosis and simultaneous spraying by drones of diverse range of crops. Our solution can detect a wide range of crop diseases, including bacterial, fungal, and viral infections. With high accuracy, even on limited hardware resources, these models empower farmers to identify and mitigate diseases in their early stages, preventing widespread damage. By providing real-time disease monitoring and classification, we help minimize crop losses, reduce pesticide usage, and support sustainable farming practices.

Environment and Sustainability

Air Quality Monitoring

Advancements in smart city technologies pave the way for innovative approaches to improving sustainability and quality of life. Integrating drones with air quality monitoring sensors offers a dynamic solution to collect real-time environmental data across urban landscapes. These drones can be deployed to monitor pollutant levels, track emission hotspots, and respond to environmental emergencies effectively. Simultaneously, embedding air quality sensors into smart street lights creates a distributed, energy-efficient network for continuous data collection. The combination of drone-based monitoring and fixed sensor networks in smart street light system enhances data accuracy and coverage, enabling better urban planning, pollution mitigation, and informed decision-making. These systems align with the goals of sustainability by promoting cleaner air, reducing health risks, and creating eco-friendly urban environments. Ultimately, this approach fosters a higher quality of life for residents and contributes significantly to the development of resilient and sustainable smart cities.

Waste Management system

A drone-assisted waste management system is a cutting-edge solution for modern urban and rural waste challenges. Drones equipped with cameras and sensors can monitor garbage levels in bins, detect illegal dumping sites, and identify pollution hotspots with real-time aerial data. This allows for precise and swift response in waste collection and disposal. Additionally, drones with load-carrying capabilities can transport waste from hard-to-access areas to designated collection points, reducing the need for human involvement in hazardous locations.

Infrastructure Inspection and Maintenance

Inspection of Power Lines and Power Grid

Drones can be transforming power grid inspection. They swiftly and efficiently access remote and hazardous areas, reducing the need for human personnel in dangerous tasks. Equipped with high-resolution cameras, drones can capture detailed images of power lines, towers, and infrastructure. This enables precise data collection on equipment conditions and potential risks, leading to faster response times and efficient issue resolution. Ultimately, our AI edge solution deployed in drones offers a cost-effective solution by reducing inspection costs and improving overall efficiency.

Detection of Structural Damages and Wear

Detecting structural damages in roads, buildings, and bridges using AI has revolutionized maintenance processes. AI-powered systems analyze high-resolution images and videos, captured by drones, to identify cracks, deformations, and surface irregularities. For potholes, AI models can process road imagery to detect and classify them based on depth and size, enabling targeted repairs. In buildings, AI algorithms analyze patterns in imagery to locate cracks, assess structural integrity, and detect potential risks like water damage or material degradation. These systems can quickly identify defects that might be missed in manual inspections. For bridges, AI-based 3D modeling and image analysis detect wear, stress fractures, and material fatigue. By processing vast datasets of historical and real-time imagery, AI can predict future structural issues, allowing for proactive maintenance. This approach eliminates human error, reduces costs, and ensures quicker, more accurate damage assessment, contributing to safer and more sustainable infrastructure.

Quality Control in Agriculture/Food

Seed Quality Assessment

The quality of seeds is a critical determinant of agricultural success. Poor seed quality leads to uneven germination and lower crop yields, resulting in financial losses for farmers. Our seed quality assessment system utilizes advanced imaging and AI-powered algorithms to analyze seed attributes such as size, shape, color, and viability. By leveraging deep learning models, we ensure that every batch of seeds is graded accurately and consistently. This technology enables the detection of damaged, immature, or defective seeds, ensuring that only the highest-quality seeds are used for planting. Farmers benefit from improved germination rates, higher yield consistency, and reduced wastage, ultimately driving greater profitability.

Food Quality and Grading System

Food quality is a key factor in consumer satisfaction and market value. Accurate grading systems ensure that fruits, vegetables, and other produce meet established quality standards. Our AI-powered food quality and grading system uses computer vision and machine learning to evaluate parameters like size, color, shape, texture, and freshness. This automated approach eliminates the subjectivity of manual grading, providing consistent and reliable results. For instance, in the grading of chilies, we assess factors like curvature, wrinkles, and brightness to classify them into Grade A, B, or C. By ensuring precision in quality assessment, we help producers command better market prices, reduce waste, and maintain customer trust.
