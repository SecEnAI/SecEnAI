Crop Disease Detection System for Agricultural Spraying Drones

Environment and Sustainability

Air Quality Monitoring System

Integrating drones with air quality monitoring sensors offers a dynamic solution to collect real-time environmental data across urban landscapes. These drones can be deployed to monitor pollutant levels, track emission hotspots, and respond to environmental emergencies effectively. Simultaneously, embedding air quality sensors into smart street lights creates a distributed, energy-efficient network for continuous data collection.

Smart Waste Management system

Drones equipped with cameras and sensors can monitor garbage levels in bins, detect illegal dumping sites, and identify pollution hotspots with real-time aerial data. This allows for precise and swift response in waste collection and disposal. Additionally, drones with load-carrying capabilities can transport waste from hard-to-access areas to designated collection points, reducing the need for human involvement in hazardous locations.

Smart Systems for Infrastructure Inspection and Maintenance

Inspection of Power Lines and Power Grid

Drones can be transforming power grid inspection. They swiftly and efficiently access remote and hazardous areas, reducing the need for human personnel in dangerous tasks. Equipped with high-resolution cameras, drones can capture detailed images of power lines, towers, and infrastructure.

Detection of Structural Damages and Wear

Detecting structural damages in roads, buildings, and bridges using AI has revolutionized maintenance processes. AI-powered systems analyze high-resolution images and videos, captured by drones, to identify cracks, deformations, and surface irregularities.

Quality Control in Agriculture/Food

Seed Quality Inspection System

Poor seed quality leads to uneven germination and lower crop yields, resulting in financial losses for farmers. Our seed quality assessment system utilizes advanced imaging and AI-powered algorithms to analyze seed attributes such as size, shape, color, and viability.

Food Quality and Grading System

Accurate grading systems ensure that fruits, vegetables, and other produce meet established quality standards. Our AI-powered food quality and grading system uses computer vision and machine learning to evaluate parameters like size, color, shape, texture, and freshness. This automated approach eliminates the subjectivity of manual grading, providing consistent and reliable results.
